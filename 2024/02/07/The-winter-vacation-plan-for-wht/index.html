The winter vacation plan for wht

The harvest of the winter camp before the Spring Festival

I have improved a lot by learning from dottle and feecle6418 for the last two weeks. I benefited a lot because of their profound knowledge and patient explanation although we’re not on the same timeline.

But I have a limited IQ and still have a lot of things that I haven’t mastered. So it`s necessary for me to start with a brief summar for a better plan.

math

This amounts to a review. I have a better understanding of number theory. I couldn’t understand the linear algebra part very well though. I hope to go ahead and gradually grasp that part as I build up my math knowledge on a regular basis.

string

As with number theory, I have a better grasp of strings.

computational geometry

I think that I showed great interest and talent in computational geometry.

data structure

There are such many parts that I could not finish all of them.

partition, dp, root sign algorithm and graph

The plan for the next few days
